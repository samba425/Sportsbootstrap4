Mission & Vision

          Hi5 Sports is a Physical and fitness Programme designed for students from Pre-Nursery to 10th Std.It has a planned curriculam through which each and every child id benefited. Every child is given equal opportiunity to participate in the activity conducted in a session by maximum utilization of the allotted slot.

      Every activity of the programme is designed to ensure that the children are having fun and simultaneously learning the fundamentals and techniques of fitness and core sports moves.In the course of the programme participants will be prepared to take up any professional sport,as they would directly or indirectly learn and praciti

Curricullam

        The programme focuses on the overall development of the participants,both in sports and physical development emphasizing on gross motor skills and fine motor skills that would directly or coincide with factors of a child's growth physically and intellectually. Our Curriculum mainly focues on the following

    . GROSS MOTOR . FINE MOTOR . BALANCE . FLEXIBILITY . LISTENING SKILL . TEAMSPIRIT . EYE-HEAD COORDINATION . EYE-FOOT COORDINATION . EYE-HEAD FOOT COORDINATION

These skills play an important role in the overall development of children.

Collabration

          His is jointly developed by physio 365 lead by Dr.Athamanathan PT(MPT Ortho and Sports),the program focuses on the overall development of the children both in sports and physical development.

      His is collaborated with SIVUS INDIA an independent NGO working for the welfare of persons with intellectual disability. Sports is one of the medium or method which is used to help persons with intellectual disability to function as independently as possible and to include them in the main-stream of the society.

      SIVUS INDIA is the only member of INAS in india and SIVUS INDIA represents india in international championships conducted by INAS .

Assessment

      Our coaches work with each child throughout the year to assess individual with their physical strengths and weaknesses. Our annual physical health report card is structured for detailed analysis of overall development of the child.
