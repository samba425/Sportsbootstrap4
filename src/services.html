Football

Football is a family of team sports that involve, to varying degrees, kicking a ball with a foot to score a goal.

BasketBall

Basketball is a handball game usually played.The objective is to get the ball through a hoop mounted high on a backboard.

Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played at the centre of which is a rectangular 22-yard-long pitch.

Khabadi

Kabaddi is a contact team sport. It is popular in South Asia and is the state game of the Indian states.

Kho kho

Kho kho is a popular tag sport from India. It is played by teams who try to avoid being touched by members of the opposing team

Badmiton

Badminton is a sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net.the most common forms of the game are "singles and "doubles".

Tennis

Tennis is a racket sport that can be played individually against a single opponent or between two teams of two players each.

Table tennis

Table tennis is a sport in which two or four players hit a lightweight ball back and forth across a table using small bats.
